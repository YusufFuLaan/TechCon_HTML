TechCon, originally known as the Tech Innovators Conference, began in 2005 in San Francisco as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and professionals. It quickly gained a reputation for being cutting-edge, focusing on emerging technologies and start-up culture. By 2011, TechCon moved to larger venues and introduced workshops, hackathons, and specialized tracks in areas like cybersecurity and AI. In 2013, it was rebranded as TechCon to reflect its broader focus. The conference expanded globally from 2016, attracting a diverse group of speakers and attendees, and began addressing social and ethical issues in technology. The 2021 conference was held entirely online due to the COVID-19 pandemic, leading to a hybrid model in subsequent years. Today, TechCon is a premier technology conference, attracting over 20,000 attendees annually and continuing to foster innovation and collaboration in the tech industry.

Mission

TechCon's goals and driving principles center on fostering innovation, collaboration, and exploration of the future of technology. The conference aims to create a dynamic platform where industry leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts can share ideas, showcase advancements, and discuss emerging trends. Committed to inclusivity and diversity, TechCon emphasizes the importance of global participation and addresses the social and ethical implications of technological developments. By offering a mix of keynote sessions, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities, TechCon strives to inspire and equip attendees to drive positive change and progress in the tech industry.

Pask Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, is a visionary entrepreneur known for pushing the boundaries of technology and innovation. With a background in physics and economics, Musk has become a central figure in the tech and space industries. He founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars, significantly advancing space exploration. At Tesla, he has revolutionized the electric vehicle market, promoting sustainable energy solutions and driving the development of autonomous driving technology. Musk’s other ventures, such as Neuralink, which aims to integrate the human brain with AI, and The Boring Company, which focuses on developing tunnel-based transportation systems, further illustrate his commitment to tackling some of the world’s most pressing challenges through innovative technology.

Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai, the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google, has been instrumental in steering the company towards new heights of innovation and success. With a background in engineering and management, Pichai joined Google in 2004 and quickly rose through the ranks, leading the development of key products like Chrome, Google Drive, and Google Apps. Under his leadership, Google has expanded its product line, improved AI capabilities, and focused on making technology more accessible and beneficial to a global audience. Pichai’s vision has driven the company’s foray into new areas such as hardware, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence, ensuring that Google remains at the forefront of technological advancement and continues to shape the future of the digital world.
